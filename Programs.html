Senior High School Tracks and Strands

Academic Track

Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand (ABM)

  • Nuture your skills to become a great business professional of the future by learning about the basic concepts of financial management, accounting, and corporate operations.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

  • The HUMSS strand is a multi-faceted option for SHS students that dream of contributing to society by way of literature, communication, medicine, education, or legal practice.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Strand (STEM)

  • Designed for students who's interests are focused on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. This strand is also recommended to those who want to polish their problem solving abilities and collaboration skills.

General Academic Strand (GAS)

  • The GAS is ideal for those who are still undecided about which career path they want to pursue in the future.
