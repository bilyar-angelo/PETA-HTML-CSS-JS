How do I enroll?



On-site Enrollment

For on-site Enrollment, please bring the following requirements:

  • Original Report Card
  • Permanent Record (Form 137)
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Photocopy of Certificate of Completion
  • 2pcs. 1x1 colored ID picture
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Long Brown Envelope


Online Enrollment

  1. Answer the enrollment form seen on the right of your screen.
  2. A response will be given to the email you have provided.




For inquiries, kindly visit our school or message use through our contacts.
